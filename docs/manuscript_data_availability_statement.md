# Manuscript availability of data and materials statement

The datasets analyzed during the current study are publicly available. Xenium spatial transcriptomic data are available from the Gene Expression Omnibus under accession GSE300147. Visium spatial transcriptomic datasets are available from the Gene Expression Omnibus under accessions GSE281978 and GSE181300. Bulk transcriptomic validation data are available from TCGA-HNSC via cBioPortal, PanCancer Atlas 2018, and from the Gene Expression Omnibus under accession GSE65858. Custom code used for preprocessing, cell-type annotation and validation, nerve-associated cell scoring, spatial zone assignment, differential expression analysis, signature scoring, survival analysis, and figure generation is available at [GitHub URL] and archived at Zenodo [DOI/link to be inserted]. Derived data objects supporting the findings of this study are available in the same repository.
